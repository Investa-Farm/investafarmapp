With **Paystack + Circle + Stellar**, you can build something that feels like a normal fintech app while using blockchain in the background.

## The Flow

### 1. Investor Deposits Money

Investor clicks:

> Deposit $100

Paystack handles:

* Card payments
* Bank transfers
* M-Pesa (through integrations)
* Mobile money

Money arrives in Investa Farm's collection account.

---

### 2. Wallet Credit

After successful payment:

Paystack webhook notifies Investa Farm.

Investa Farm then:

* Credits investor wallet balance
* Creates equivalent USDC value in Circle

Example:

Investor deposits $100

↓

Wallet Balance = $100

↓

100 USDC held in Circle

The investor simply sees:

**Wallet Balance: $100**

not blockchain addresses.

---

### 3. Investor Buys Farm Shares

Investor purchases:

* 10 Tomato Shares @ $10

Circle:

* Deducts $100 from wallet

Stellar:

* Records ownership of 10 shares

Result:

**Circle = Money**

**Stellar = Ownership**

---

### 4. Mid-Season Trading

Suppose:

* Crop health improves
* Weather outlook is favorable
* Market demand rises

Your pricing engine updates share value.

Example:

Share price:

$10 → $12

Investor wants to exit.

A new investor buys.

Circle transfers:

$120

Stellar transfers:

10 shares

Ownership updates instantly.

---

### 5. Harvest Revenue

Revenue from buyers/offtakers:

$10,000

Investa Farm calculates:

* Farmers: 55%
* Investors: 20%
* Investa Farm: 25%

Circle distributes funds.

Example:

Investor owns 5% of investor pool.

Investor receives:

$100 USDC

directly into wallet.

---

# Where Stellar Helps

Without Stellar:

You would only have:

* Wallet balances
* Database ownership records

This works but creates trust issues.

---

With Stellar:

### 1. Verifiable Ownership

Investor truly owns:

* Tomato Farm Shares
* Maize Farm Shares

Ownership exists on-chain.

Not just in your database.

---

### 2. Secondary Market

Stellar makes it easy to:

* Buy shares
* Sell shares
* Transfer shares

just like stocks.

Without Stellar:

You'd have to build a complete ownership ledger yourself.

---

### 3. Transparency

Every share transfer:

* Timestamped
* Recorded
* Auditable

Investors trust the system more.

---

### 4. Global Investors

Investor from:

* Kenya
* Nigeria
* UK
* USA

can own the same farm shares.

No need for local banking infrastructure.

---

### 5. Future Tokenized Portfolios

Later:

Instead of buying one farm:

Investor buys:

* Maize Portfolio
* Export Portfolio
* Livestock Portfolio

These portfolio units can also be Stellar assets.
