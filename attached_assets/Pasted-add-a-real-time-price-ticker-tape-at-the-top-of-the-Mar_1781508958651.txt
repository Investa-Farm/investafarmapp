You input your goals (e.g., target return: 20%, risk tolerance: medium, preferred crop: maize or beans, investment duration: 4 months). AI scans all active farms, matches you to the best-fit opportunities, and even auto-invests new deposits from M-Pesa or card.Yield Prediction Engine
AI ingests satellite imagery, historical weather data, soil sensors, and crop health reports. It outputs a probabilistic yield forecast (e.g., “85% chance of achieving 22–28% return”). This is updated weekly as the season progresses.Risk Scoring
Each farm gets a dynamic risk score (1–10) based on real-time factors: drought risk, pest outbreaks, farm manager track record, input quality. Investors can filter by score or let AI avoid high-risk farms automatically.Reinvestment Automation
After harvest, you can set rules: “Reinvest 70% of my earnings into similar farms, send 30% to M-Pesa.” AI rebalances your portfolio across crops and regions to reduce seasonality risk.Simulation & “What If” Tools
Before committing, test scenarios: “What if rainfall is 20% below average?” or “What if maize prices drop 10%?” AI models the impact on your final return.Benefit: Passive investors get hands‑off, optimized exposure.
