# Investa Farm Farmer Journey & Platform Specification

Build the farmer experience as a complete farm financing and agricultural investment ecosystem, not as a traditional loan application.

## Farmer Registration

Create a multi-step onboarding flow.

### Personal Information

Required Fields:

* Full Name
* National ID Number
* Phone Number
* Email Address
* Gender
* Date of Birth

### Location Information

Required Fields:

* Country
* County
* Sub-County
* Ward
* Village
* Nearest Trading Center
* Nearest Landmark
* GPS Coordinates (Auto Capture)
* Pin Farm Location on Map

Purpose:

* Farm mapping
* Satellite analysis
* Risk assessment
* Extension support
* Investor transparency

---

## KYC Verification

Farmer uploads:

* ID Front
* ID Back
* Selfie Verification

Optional:

* Title Deed
* Lease Agreement
* Cooperative Membership

Statuses:

* Pending Verification
* Verified
* Rejected

---

## Credit Scoring Engine

Automatically generate:

### Identity Score

Based on:

* KYC verification
* Contact verification

### Farm Score

Based on:

* Acreage
* Historical performance
* Satellite imagery
* Crop suitability

### Risk Score

Based on:

* Weather
* Crop type
* Historical yields
* Market demand

Output:

Funding Eligibility

Example:

Funding Limit:
$2,500

Farmer Score:
82/100
