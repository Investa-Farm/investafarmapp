Collection: order_book

Fields: farm_id, investor_id, side ('buy'/'sell'), quantity, limit_price_usd, status ('open'/'filled'/'cancelled'/'partial'), created_at, updated_at

Indexes: (farm_id, side, limit_price_usd, created_at) for fast matching

3. Matching Engine (Background Worker):

Run continuously (every 5 seconds) for all farms with open orders.

Fetch open buys (highest price first) and open sells (lowest price first).

While highest bid ≥ lowest ask, execute trade at ask price.

Fill order quantities, mark as filled or partial.

Record each trade in a trades collection with: farm_id, buyer_id, seller_id, quantity, price_usd, total_usd, buyer_fee, seller_fee, created_at.

4. Stellar Settlement:

After a trade is recorded, execute two Stellar payments:

USDC from buyer → seller (minus seller fee).

FARM asset from seller → buyer (quantity = traded shares).

Store stellar_tx_hash in the trade record.

If settlement fails, mark trade as pending_settlement and retry.

5. No‑Arbitrage AI Check (GET /api/secondary/arbitrage/{farm_id}):

Compare current mid‑market price with fair value.

If deviation > 5%, flag arbitrage_opportunity: true with signal: "BUY" or "SELL".

Push notification to relevant investors.

6. Investor Endpoints:

POST /api/order – place limit order (buy/sell).

DELETE /api/order/{order_id} – cancel open order.

GET /api/order/history/{investor_id} – past orders and trades.

GET /api/trades/{farm_id} – recent trades for a farm (price discovery).

This secondary market engine turns Investa Farm into a full‑fledged exchange.
